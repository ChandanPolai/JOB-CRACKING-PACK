Mere senior, agar SQL se related interview ki baat karein, toh **4 sabse important cheezein** jo aksar poochi jaati hain, wo hain:  

---

### **1. Joins**  
- **Why Important:**  
  Joins ka use multiple tables ke data ko combine karne ke liye hota hai, aur ye har real-world database ka base hota hai.  

- **Topics to Prepare:**  
  - Inner Join  
  - Left Join  
  - Right Join  
  - Full Outer Join  
  - Self Join  

  

- **Example Question:**  
  "Explain the difference between Inner Join and Left Join with an example."  

---

### **2. Indexes**  
- **Why Important:**  
  Indexes query execution ko faster banate hain, aur performance tuning ka ek key part hain.  

- **Topics to Prepare:**  
  - Clustered Index vs Non-Clustered Index  
  - How indexes improve performance  
  - Index ka impact on Insert/Update/Delete operations  

- **Example Question:**  
  "What are clustered and non-clustered indexes? How do they differ?"  

---

### **3. Views and Triggers**  
- **Why Important:**  
  Views aur Triggers ka use data abstraction aur automation ke liye hota hai. Ye advanced concepts hain jo often mid-to-advanced level interviews me pooche jaate hain.  

- **Topics to Prepare:**  
  - View ke types (Simple vs Complex)  
  - Trigger ka kaam aur types (Before vs After)  
  - Real-life examples (security and auditing ke liye)  

- **Example Question:**  
  "What are Triggers? Explain with an example of BEFORE INSERT Trigger."  

---

### **4. Transactions**  
- **Why Important:**  
  Transactions ensure data integrity aur atomicity ko maintain karte hain, jo ki kisi bhi critical application ke liye zaroori hai.  

- **Topics to Prepare:**  
  - ACID properties (Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability)  
  - COMMIT, ROLLBACK, SAVEPOINT  
  - Transaction isolation levels (Read Uncommitted, Read Committed, Repeatable Read, Serializable)  

- **Example Question:**  
  "Explain the ACID properties of a transaction. Why are they important?"  

---

### **Pro Tip:**  
- SQL interview me aksar **query writing** ka question zaroor hota hai.  
  Example:  
  - "Write a query to find the second highest salary in an Employee table."  
  - "Find duplicate records in a table and delete them."  

- Query optimization aur **Explain Plan** ka concept bhi basic level par tayar rakhna zaroori hai.
